Frequently asked.

The questions we hear before almost every engagement begins.

What's the minimum budget you work with?

Most engagements start once monthly ad spend reaches a level where testing across channels is statistically meaningful — we'll tell you plainly on the call if you're not there yet.

How soon will we see results?

Early signal typically appears within the first few weeks of testing; durable, scalable performance is usually a one-to-two quarter build. We won't promise a faster timeline than that.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We ask for enough runway to test properly, but the relationship continues because of results, not a lock-in period.

Why don't you show client results yet?

Because we're a new agency and we'd rather be honest about that than publish borrowed numbers. Your success story could be the first one on this page.
